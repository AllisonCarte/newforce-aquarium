Tank Care Tips

Tank Cleaning
Test your water for vital parameters.
Clean the aquarium walls.
Start from the bottom upward and rinse filter floss or scrubber frequently.
Vacuum the gravel.
Change 10-15% of the water.
Rinse filter inserts with the extracted water.
Salinity
Salinity is measured in parts per trillion and varies from species to species.
Water temperature
The ideal water temperature varies from species to species.
